Our Story

Founded in 2008 in Quanzhou, Fujian Province, AquaGrip Bags began as a small family business with a vision to revolutionize beach accessories. What started as a local manufacturer has grown into a leading B2B supplier of premium EVA beach bags, serving clients across the globe.

Our journey has been driven by innovation, quality, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Today, we proudly manufacture over 500,000 bags annually, each crafted with precision and attention to detail.
