Product Description

The Mini EVA Beach Bag is designed for those who prefer to travel light while keeping their essentials safe and dry. This compact version of our popular beach bag maintains all the waterproof benefits in a smaller, more portable package.

Key Features:

  • Compact Size: Perfect for carrying just the essentials without bulk
  • Waterproof Protection: Complete protection against water, sand, and moisture
  • Ultra Lightweight: At only 180g, you'll barely notice you're carrying it
  • Easy Storage: Folds flat when not in use for convenient storage
  • Quick Access: Wide opening for easy access to contents
  • Durable Material: High-quality EVA resists wear and tear
